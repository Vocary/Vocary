What makes Vocary different

Built for words that stick

📖
Your Personal Dictionary
Add any English word and get an instant rich definition, etymology, memory hooks, example sentences, synonyms by difficulty level and CEFR rating. Your vocabulary, beautifully organised.
🧠
Spaced Repetition Learning
Vocary's seven-session practice schedule surfaces words at exactly the right moment: Day 1, 2, 4, 7, 14, 30 and 60. Science-backed, effortless to follow. Words move from new to mastered.
☀️
Word of the Day
Every category has its own daily word of the day, covering Business, Psychology, Law, Philosophy and more. A fresh word every morning resets at your local midnight. A tiny habit with a big payoff.
⚡
Daily Practice Sessions
Fill-in-the-blank, definition matching, example sentence challenges. Each session is short, sharp and varied, designed so no two practice sessions feel the same.
📈
Track Your Progress
Streaks, XP, levels and a live leaderboard keep you accountable. Watch your vocabulary score climb as words move from Learning to Proficient to Mastered.
✨
Discover New Words
Browse curated word decks across 23 categories, from Business and Science to Philosophy and Arts. Swipe through, save what matters, skip what doesn't.

Three steps to a richer vocabulary

1
Add a word
Type any word and Vocary instantly generates a complete definition, memory hook, etymology, example sentences at your level, synonyms and more. No more shallow dictionary lookups.
2
Practice daily
Each word follows a seven-session learning schedule. Short daily challenges, never more than a few minutes, that space out perfectly to move words into long-term memory.
3
Own it forever
Once a word reaches Mastered it's yours. Track your growing vocabulary, climb the leaderboard, and discover new words every day across categories that match your interests.
